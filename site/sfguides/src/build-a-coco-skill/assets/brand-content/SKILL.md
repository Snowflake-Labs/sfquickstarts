# Brand Content

Generate marketing copy that adheres to Peak Outfitters' brand voice and style guidelines.

## When to Use

Use this skill whenever a teammate needs customer-facing marketing copy – a product
announcement email or a slide/deck outline. It exists so anyone can produce on-brand
content without memorizing the style guide.

## Setup

**Load** `references/brand-guidelines.md` before drafting. It contains the voice, tone,
approved terminology, and formatting rules that every piece of content must follow.

## Workflow

### Step 1: Gather the brief

Ask the user for:

1. **Content type**: announcement email or slide outline
2. **Product or topic**: what is being announced or presented
3. **Audience**: e.g., existing customers, prospects, retail partners
4. **Key points**: 2–4 things the content must communicate

**⚠️ STOP**: Confirm the brief before drafting so you don't waste effort on the wrong angle.

### Step 2: Draft against the guidelines

Draft the requested content, applying `references/brand-guidelines.md`:

- Open with the approved brand hook, not a generic greeting.
- Use the sanctioned voice (confident, plainspoken, outdoors-forward) and terminology.
- Keep paragraphs short; lead with the customer benefit before the feature.
- End with the standard call to action for the content type.

### Step 3: Present with a compliance note

Return the draft, then add a short "Brand check" list showing which guideline rules you
applied (voice, terminology, structure, CTA). This makes it easy for the user to verify
the copy is on-brand before publishing.

**⚠️ STOP**: Final review with the user.

## Stopping Points

- ✋ Step 1: Brief confirmed
- ✋ Step 3: Final review before the content is used

## Output

An on-brand announcement email or slide outline, plus a short brand-compliance checklist.
