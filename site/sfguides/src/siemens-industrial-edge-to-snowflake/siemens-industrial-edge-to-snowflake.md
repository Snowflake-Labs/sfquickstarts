# Siemens Industrial Edge to Snowflake
<!-- ------------------------ -->
## Detailed View of Snowflake / Siemens Industrial Edge: Leveraging Siemens Access to the Shopfloor

![quickstart reference architecture](assets/image.coreimg.svg)

Siemens Industrial Edge is an edge/on-prem platform, offering functionalities like edge device management and connectors. Connectors are deployed via a Siemens marketplace and centrally managed on the edge through the common configurator.

**2** - Common Configurator transfers data via the Snowflake connector that subscribes to topics and creates tags.

**3**  - Raw data landed in Snowflake

**4** - Build harmonized data layer, mapping time series, orders, bill of materials into a process digital twin.

**5** - Perform analytical operations on harmonized data layer.
